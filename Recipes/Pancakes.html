    Here's what you need to make pancakes.
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablepoons sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat a griddle to medium high heat.
  2. Get your favorite mixing bowl. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in said bowl.
  3. Grab your favorite measuring cup.
  4. Combine milk, egg, and oil.
  5. Combine the wet and the dry ingredients and mix.
  6. Scoop the pancake mix onto the griddle in 1/4 cupfuls
  7. Combine the confectioners sugar, milk, vanilla, and butter.
  8. flip the pancakes over after they're cooked on one side
